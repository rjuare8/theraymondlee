About Me

Raymond L. Costello Jr grew up on the south side of Chicago in the vibrant neighborhood of Pilsen, where the hustle and creativity are as real as the deep-dish pizza debates. He fell into the jewelry world in April 2011 and hasn’t looked back since, proudly “Putting Rings on Tings" for over a decade. After five years of working for someone else, he decided to take a shot at going solo, opening his first store in 2016 and his second company not too long afterwards in 2022.

As a first-generation business owner in a field often run by multi-generational families, Raymond Lee carved out his niche in engagement rings and bridal jewelry while mastering the art of creating timeless pieces For Everyone / Everywhere. He’s proof that you don’t need a family legacy to create magic—you just need a vision, a good sense of humor, and maybe a little coffee. His laid-back, one-on-one approach has made him the go-to jeweler for clients who want more than just sparkle—they want a story.
